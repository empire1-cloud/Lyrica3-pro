## 📰 PRESS RELEASE

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Lyrica3 Pro Launches Digital Birth Certificates and Micro-Royalty Distribution for AI Music**

*First Platform to Provide Full Transparency and Fair Compensation for AI Co-Produced Music*

**El Monte, CA — May 16, 2026** — Lyrica3 Pro today announced the launch of the first AI music platform with digital birth certificates and micro-royalty distribution, solving the attribution and payment crisis in AI-generated music.

**THE PROBLEM:**
As AI tools like Suno, Udio, and LIA become mainstream in music production, a critical question remains unanswered: When AI is a co-producer, who owns the output? Who gets paid?

Traditional platforms provide no transparency into which AI models were used, what training data powered them, or how royalties should be split. This creates legal ambiguity for creators, distributors, and copyright offices.

**THE SOLUTION:**
Lyrica3 Pro introduces two groundbreaking innovations:

**1. Digital Birth Certificates**
Every track generated on Lyrica3 Pro includes a tamper-proof digital birth certificate showing:
- AI models used (name, version, provider)
- Training data sources (e.g., "MusicGen: public domain, Demucs: licensed")
- Human creative decisions (prompt, edits, parameters)
- Royalty split percentages for all stakeholders

The certificate is cryptographically hashed (SHA-256), timestamped, and stored immutably. Creators can export it as JSON/PDF or embed it in audio file metadata (ID3 tags).

**2. Micro-Royalty Distribution**
Lyrica3 Pro operates a 7-stakeholder payment system that automatically distributes royalties via Stripe Connect:

- **Prompt Writer** (40%): Creative direction
- **Vocal Owner** (20%): Voice/timbre
- **Emotional Arc Designer** (10%): Biometric artifact design
- **Persona Creator** (10%): Cultural/sonic identity
- **Producer** (10%): Final arrangement
- **Remixer** (5%): Stem transformation
- **AI Model Owners** (5%): Model development (split among Meta, Spotify, etc.)

Every contribution gets attribution. Every stakeholder gets paid. Every output becomes a revenue-bearing asset.

**THE TECHNOLOGY:**
Lyrica3 Pro uses open-source AI models (MusicGen from Meta, Demucs from Meta, Pedalboard from Spotify) orchestrated via Google Cloud Vertex AI agents. The platform generates:
- Natural language → MIDI melody with biometric artifacts
- 4 audio stems (drums, bass, melody, vocals)
- Analog warmth processing (tape saturation, acid delay)
- Cultural tuning (SGV Chicano: E♭ Major/C Minor, 72 BPM)

Cost per track: $0.0001-$0.001 (vs $0.008-$0.02 for competitors)

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
"AI is no longer a generator — it's a co-producer," said shiestybizz113-cell, founder of Lyrica3 Pro. "And today, we're launching the infrastructure that makes AI music creator-owned. Every contribution gets attribution. Every stakeholder gets paid. This is the future of the creator economy."

As platforms like Veena Studio demonstrate, AI is shifting from novelty to practical co-producer. Lyrica3 Pro provides the economic and legal infrastructure this shift requires.
