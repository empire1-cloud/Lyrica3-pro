**Lyrica3** is the first AI music engine with **slang-aware phonetic transformations**.

#### What Makes Us Different?

**1. Barrio Phonetics Engine™**
- Detects slang terms in lyrics (homeboy, finna, innit, saranghae, wetin)
- Maps slang → IPA targets → DSP parameters
- Applies 3-tier transformations:
  - **Tier 1: Vowel Shifts** (home /hoʊm/ → /hɔːm/ warmer)
  - **Tier 2: Consonant Texture** (T-glottalization, D-softening, flapped T)
  - **Tier 3: Rhythmic Compression** (gonna 2 syllables → 1 syllable, 30% faster)

**2. 7 Global Cultural Lenses**
- 🌵 **Chicano Soul** (SGV Oldies, homeboy/ese slang, requinto guitar)
- 🌙 **Trap Soul** (Late-night honesty, finna/tryna slang, 808 sub-bass)
- 🎤 **Boom Bap** (NYC 90s hip-hop, yo/aight slang, SP-1200 drums)
- 🌍 **Lagos Pulse** (Afrobeat, wetin/oya Pidgin English + Yoruba)
- ✨ **Seoul Shine** (K-Pop, saranghae/oppa Korean, crystal-clear vocals)
- 🌫️ **London Fog** (UK Drill, innit/bruv MLE, glottal stops on all T's)
- 🔥 **Neo-Soul** (D'Angelo/Erykah vibes, organic soulful groove)

**3. Cultural Gatekeeping Protocol**
- Blocks culturally incompatible slang (e.g., "ese" in K-Pop → WARNING)
- Respects cultural origins, prevents appropriation
- Community-verified slang dictionary (v2.0.0, 40+ entries)

**4. VICS (Vibe Interpretation & Cultural Synthesis)**
- Emotional intelligence layer detects subtext in lyrics
- Maps emotional vectors (valence, arousal, tension, cultural resonance)
- Selects culturally-appropriate vocal archetypes (11 historical styles)
- Generates DSP parameters (formant shifts, timing offsets, biometric artifacts)

**5. Explainable AI**
- Before/after IPA transformations shown in real-time
- CSN (Cognitive Synthesis Network) explains WHY choices were made
- Full VICS blueprint included with every track

## FAQ

**Q: How is this different from Suno/Udio?**
A: Suno/Udio have NO slang detection or phonetic transformation. We have video proof: Suno sang JSON as lyrics. Lyrica3 has 40+ slang entries with IPA targets → DSP parameters. We detect "homeboy" and transform /hoʊm.bɔɪ/ → /hɔːm.bɔɪ/ with -0.15 formant shift + vocal fry grit.

**Q: What's an IPA target?**
A: IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet) is the standard for representing pronunciation. "homeboy" in textbook English = /hoʊm.bɔɪ/. In Chicano Soul = /hɔːm.bɔɪ/ (warmer, flattened vowel). We map slang → IPA → DSP parameters (formant shift, timing offset, biometric artifacts).

**Q: Can I add my own slang?**
A: Yes! Pro plan users can submit slang entries for community verification. If approved, your slang gets added to the dictionary with IPA targets + cultural notes.

**Q: What if I use slang from the wrong cultural lens?**
A: Cultural Gatekeeping Protocol blocks it. Example: "ese" (Chicano) in K-Pop → WARNING. "Saranghae" (Korean) in Chicano Soul → INCOMPATIBLE. Respects cultural origins.

**Q: How accurate is the slang detection?**
A: 92% accuracy on community-verified slang. False positives handled via context (e.g., "right" as direction vs "right" as affirmation). Continuously improving via user feedback.

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**
A: Free tier: personal use only. Pro plan: commercial usage rights included. Enterprise: white-label API with custom licensing.

**Q: What's VICS v3.0?**
A: Vibe Interpretation & Cultural Synthesis. 5-organ emotional intelligence pipeline: AURA (emotional analysis) → EFL (cultural forensics) → ASE (slang detection) → ECHO (performance directives) → EFAD (final mix). Prevents generic output.

**Q: Why 7 cultural lenses?**
A: We started with Chicano Soul (our founder's heritage) and expanded to underserved markets in AI music: Lagos Afrobeat (Pidgin English), Seoul K-Pop (Korean/Konglish), London Drill (MLE). Specificity is sovereignty.

**Q: How do you prevent cultural appropriation?**
A: Cultural Gatekeeping Protocol + community verification. Slang dictionary includes cultural notes, reference artists, and incompatible lens warnings. We don't monetize culture we don't understand.
